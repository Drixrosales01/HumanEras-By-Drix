The Industrial Era, spanning the 18th to the early 20th century, was marked by a period of significant technological, socioeconomic, and cultural changes. It began with the mechanization of the textile industries, the development of iron-making techniques, and the rise of the steam engine. This era transformed largely rural, agrarian societies into industrial urban areas, leading to unprecedented growth and advancement.
