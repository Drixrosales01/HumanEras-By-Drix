The Prehistoric Era refers to all the time before recorded history began. It spans from the time when Earth first formed, more than 4.5 billion years ago, up until about 5,000 years ago when humans began to write down their own history. This was a time of evolving and adapting for life on Earth, leading to the rise of the dinosaurs, their extinction, and eventually the emergence of humans.
